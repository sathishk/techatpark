TECH@PARK is a non-profit organization that is committed to empowering underprivileged freshers by providing them with training and resources to succeed in the IT industry. Our approach to training is unique and designed to produce top-quality talents who are valuable assets to any hirer. Our students are chosen based on their potential, trained by industry experts, and are technically productive from day one. As a result, TECH@PARK alumni are highly sought-after by employers, making them an excellent addition to any team.

  • Quality Over Quantity

    At TECH@PARK, we believe in quality over quantity. That's why we take a rigorous approach to selecting our students, handpicking only the best and most deserving candidates. When a candidate is accepted into TECH@PARK, it means they have demonstrated the potential and drive to succeed in the IT industry.

  • Innovative Training Methods

    TECH@PARK's training methods are always evolving to keep up with the latest market needs and trends. Our students are professionally trained by industry experts, ensuring they are equipped with the most current and relevant knowledge and skills. This innovative approach to training makes TECH@PARK's students stand out as valuable assets for any hirer.

  • Instantly Productive

    TECH@PARK's unique training approach ensures that our students are technically productive from day one. We focus on practical, hands-on learning that prepares our students for the challenges of the IT industry. This means that our students require minimal onboarding time and can start contributing to projects right away, making them a valuable asset for any hirer.
